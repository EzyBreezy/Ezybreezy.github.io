A little about me...

My name is Ezana Bahta a Developer from the Washington DC area. I graduated from General Assembly Bootcamp (GAB) November of 2018. Currently, I am applying what I learned through GAB's curriculum and building projects such as Visual Board applications to help keep your eyes on the prize. I am continuing to build on my experience and skills by gettings certified through the FreeCodeCamp Curriculum.

Prior to becoming a developer, I spent a lot of time in the Sales industry. Most recently, I was a manager at a Sprint Retail Store. There, I led my team to be one of the top performing stores in the Northern Virginia District. I also performed residential Heating, Ventilation and Air conditioning (HVAC) sales for one of the largest HVAC Companies In the US. In addition, I also worked with numbers while serving as a tax preparer in the Philadelphia Market.

When I am not coding, I am usually working on maintaining and detailing cars. I also enjoy spending time with friends and tackling a nifty project, such as those from instructables. I really enjoy do it your own (DIY) projects. Seeing challenging projects through gives me a sense of fulfillment. I have curiosity that motivates investigating and study and these projects allow me to do that. In conjunction. I have also picked up a hobby with drones. I build them, fly them and race them. I am very hands-on and hope the skills I have acquired through my study and hobbies are able to assist you!
